iConnect

iConnect is a complete suite of apps and services which allows assists in digitising various processes in an educational institution. Powered by 24×7 back-end servers, students, faculty and administrators can access the system from a wide range of personal devices. From smartphones and tablets running on Android platform, to Microsoft Surface tablets running Windows 8.1 operating system, iConnect has support for many different devices. Apps are available on various leading app stores, including the Nokia X appstore and CNET Download.com. For other systems, an user can always access the portal through a unified website, hosted at http://souravghosh.cloudapp.net. From the students’ point of view, this portal is a platform where they can share feedback about their courses. The information is aggregated and made available to the concerned subject teacher who can use this information to improve the method of organising classes. Anonymity ensures that students are able to give feedback without personal identification. Only students who attend a predefined threshold of classes are eligible for this opportunity. Faculty members, on the other hand, can access and monitor submissions and queries of students and guide them occasionally using their personal devices. They can also upload assignments and study materials for a select group of students. Messages may be exchanged among users through individual inboxes and mailing lists. System administrators may add, modify and remove users, courses, and relationships between them in a few clicks.

This project has been developed by my team – Neelakshi Das, Krishanu Ganguli, Krishna Kamal Pal, and myself, Sourav Ghosh, as our project for IBM The Great Mind Challenge 2012. We put in a lot of effort to create a secure and robust site which intends to minimize corruption by cross-checking details with various authorized sources. The final round results of the same were declared on 16th September, 2013 and we were really excited to know that we had been declared to be among the winners of IBM TGMC 2012! Find our team Apocalypse at http://bit.ly/ibm_tgmc In this project, a Tenders Management System has been designed for the Public Works Department, Government of India. Separate portals are available for different categories of users – Registered Companies, PWD Officers, Administrator, and Guests. Registration of companies is completed only after the registration documents have been successfully verified by authorities such as the Police Department, Income Tax Department, Bank, etc. Companies can view upcoming tenders grouped in various relevant categories, and submit tenders. The administrator is responsible for managing tender documents and floating new tenders on behalf of the Government, in addition to other usual responsibilities.

Slide 'em Up

Slide ’em Up is one of my first apps published on the Windows Store. It is a classic 8-puzzle game with custom tiles. This is a simple game, tailored for full-screen game playing as well as different snap sizes supported on devices running Windows 8 and Windows 8.1. The player’s mission is to arrange a jumbled set of tiles to form the picture shown as a reference. A tile can be moved horizontally and vertically to take the place of an adjoining empty slot. The number of movements and time taken contribute to the player’s score. The game can be downloaded on devices running Windows 8, Windows 8 RT and Windows 8.1 from http://apps.microsoft.com/webpdp/en-us/app/slide-em-up/d27f464e-9455-4e6d-a1cd-87e7c77182b6 [UPDATE: This app has been rated 10 stars (out of 10) by BestWindows8Apps.net and has been featured as Editor’s Pick as of 29th Nov, 2013. Durga Puja

Durga Puja (Bengali: দুর্গা পূজা), also referred to as Durgotsava, ‘Festival of Durga’ or Sharadotsav is an annual Hindu festival in South Asia that celebrates worship of the Hindu goddess Durga. It refers to all the six days observed as Mahalaya, Shashthi, Maha Saptami, Maha Ashtami, Maha Navami and Bijoya Dashami. The dates of Durga Puja celebrations are set according to the traditional Hindu calendar and the fortnight corresponding to the festival is called Devi Paksha (Bengali: দেবী পক্ষ, ‘Fortnight of the Goddess’). Devi Paksha is preceded by Mahalaya (Bengali: মহালয়া), the last day of the previous fortnight Pitri Paksha, ‘Fortnight of the Forefathers’), and is ended on Kojagori Lokkhi Puja (‘Worship of Goddess Lakshmi on Kojagori Full Moon Night’). Durga Puja festival marks the victory of Goddess Durga over the evil buffalo demon Mahishasura. Thus, Durga Puja festival epitomises the victory of Good over Evil. This application brings you detailed information about the legend, history, celebration, rituals of Durga Puja. It also enlists recipes of some of the popular cuisines associated with this festival. Some of the prominent ‘Sarbojonin Durga Pujas’ and a few Pujas organised overseas have also been mentioned. MuzikSpot

This project was developed by my team – Neelakshi Das, Krishanu Ganguli, Krishna Kamal Pal, and me, Sourav Ghosh, for completion of our Summer Training under Oriens Infotech Pvt. Ltd. in the month of June 2013. Our project was appreciated as one of the best projects and an industry-ready one, which really means a lot for us! MuzikSpot is an online music store from where the music of many popular genres can be downloaded without having to pay for them, i.e. it is a free download site. It has six genres-Bollywood, Pop, Rock/Metal, Blues, and Jazz & Electronica. A customer can download songs only if he/she is a registered user. Any user who is not registered can only explore the store search for, and play the songs but may not download. Registered artists can upload their albums and songs. They can check the number of downloads of their songs and also the number of uploaded songs. A special feature has been implemented in this project called MEI (Music Experience Index). MEI shows the ratio of the total number of downloads to the total number of uploads rated on a scale of 10. The admin is responsible for maintaining the site, keeping records of all the users and artists, approving new registrations and upgrading the system.
