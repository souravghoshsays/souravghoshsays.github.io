About Me

About Me

সৌরভ ঘোষ   सौरभ घोष   곳소우럽   ಸೌರಭ್ ಘೋಷ್

Aspiring computer scientist. Loves to read and listen to audiobooks. | On-device AI, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Generative AI

[Career Timeline] I am currently working with Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore as a Chief Engineer. I received my M.Tech degree in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur in 2017 and B.Tech degree from West Bengal University of Technology in 2014. I had the privilege of being supervised by Prof. Soumya K Ghosh during the drafting of my M.Tech thesis, and of being advised by Prof. Piyal Sarkar during the conduct of my B.Tech project. Earlier in my career, I have worked as an Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Developer with Tata Consultancy Services, and have served as a Microsoft Student Partner (MSP).

[Intellectual Property Creation] I have published 15+ research papers in peer-reviewed forums, leading to 1 best paper award and 2 nominations, and have filed 7+ patents (CS completed). I have served as a reviewer and program comittee member for over 10 international conferences, including ACL, NeurIPS, EMNLP, ICCV, ACL-IJCNLP, NAACL-HLT, ICON, CSAE, and have reviewed over 40 manuscripts. I also participate in reviewing submissions at Samsung Best Paper Award, where I received the Best Reviewer award (On-device AI) in 2023. My current research interests include on-device AI, natural language processing, generative AI, computer vision.

[Research-to-Market] I have played an active role in the development and successful commercialization of Mass-market Solutions, like multilingual input method (Alive Intelligence), on-device Indic language models, Flagship solutions like On-device Learning Framework, Advanced Smart Reply, as well as B2B Solutions for airline digital marketing, defence communications, in-cabin experiences, etc. I have also served as the Software Competency Catalyst and Expert Task Force Champion for KB Part.

[Mentorship and Guest Roles] I have mentored over 35 teams as part of Samsung PRISM, an industry-academia collaboration involving eminent professors and students from leading Indian institutes. I have served as a Judge at Smart India Hackathon, hosted by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, for two consecutive years. I have been a founder of InspirIT, the technology club of the Department of Information Technology at my alma mater, where as an active member, I have organized and presented at technical sessions. It has been a pleasure to be assoicated with InspirIT for over a decade, where I have returned as a Judge or Alumni Guest at events.

[Certifications, Awards, Miscellaneous] While the focus of my recent work has been on generative AI, large language models, and multimodal AI, I have experience in cloud computing, data analytics, web/app development, content management solutions. I enjoy writing blog posts that include both technical and works of fiction. With encouragement from friends, at times I share amateur portrait sketches on social media. I hold Samsung Software Competency (Professional) and Microsoft Certified Professional certifications. Over the years, I have received Samsung Excellence Award - Innovator (Publications), Samsung Citizen Awards (x2), TCS LIREL Honor Rolls, Star of LG, IBM TGMC Winners Award, among others.
